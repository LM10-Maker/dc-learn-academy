Cookie Policy

DC-LEARN-COOK v1.0.0 · Legacy Business Engineers Ltd · 26 Mar 2026
Plain English Summary We use strictly necessary cookies to keep you logged in and remember your progress. We don’t use advertising cookies or sell data to third parties. You can’t turn off strictly necessary cookies without breaking the platform.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your login status and preferences.

2. Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the DC-LEARN platform to function. Without them, you cannot log in, access paid modules, or have your quiz progress saved. You cannot opt out of strictly necessary cookies while using the platform.

Cookie Purpose Duration
sb-access-token Supabase authentication — keeps you logged in Session / 1 hour
sb-refresh-token Refreshes your session without requiring re-login 30 days
dc_theme Remembers your dark/light theme preference 1 year

Analytics Cookies

DC-LEARN does not currently use third-party analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics). LBE uses Supabase’s built-in query logs for aggregate usage analysis (e.g., which modules are accessed most frequently). This data is not linked to individual identities and is not shared with third parties.

Payment Cookies

The Stripe checkout flow (external to DC-LEARN) sets cookies necessary to process your payment securely. These are governed by Stripe’s Privacy Policy.

Advertising Cookies

DC-LEARN does not use advertising cookies. LBE does not use retargeting pixels, social media tracking cookies, or behavioural advertising on the DC-LEARN platform.

3. How to Manage Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from logging into DC-LEARN.

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

4. Changes to this Policy

LBE may update this Cookie Policy if the platform adds new functionality requiring additional cookies. Material changes will be communicated with 14 days’ notice via email to registered users.
