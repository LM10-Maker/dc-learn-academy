Privacy Policy

DC-LEARN-PRIV v1.0.0 · Legacy Business Engineers Ltd · 26 Mar 2026
Plain English Summary We collect your email and progress data to run the programme and issue certificates. We use Stripe to process payments — we never see your card details. We don’t sell your data. You can request deletion at any time. We comply with GDPR.

1. Who We Are

Legacy Business Engineers Ltd (Company No. [Reg No.]), Dublin, Ireland, is the data controller for personal data processed in connection with DC-LEARN. Contact: hello@legacybe.ie.

This policy applies to personal data collected through the DC-LEARN platform at learn.legacybe.ie and legacybe.ie.

2. What Data We Collect

Account data

  • Email address (used for login, certificate delivery, and programme communications)
  • Name (used on CPD certificates — provided at certificate generation, not at signup)
  • Organisation name (Corporate/Enterprise purchases only)

Progress and assessment data

  • Module access timestamps
  • Quiz responses and scores
  • Level completion status
  • Certificate issuance records

Payment data

Payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. LBE does not receive or store card numbers, CVV codes, or bank account details. LBE receives a Stripe customer ID and transaction confirmation. Stripe’s privacy policy: stripe.com/ie/privacy.

Technical data

  • IP address (stored by Supabase as part of authentication logs)
  • Browser type and OS (standard server logs, not linked to personal identity)
  • Session duration (aggregated, not linked to individual accounts)

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) as retained and applied in Ireland, LBE processes your data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract — processing necessary to deliver the Programme you have purchased
  • Legitimate interests — understanding how the Programme is used to improve content quality
  • Consent — marketing communications (opt-in only; you may withdraw at any time)

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To track your progress and unlock modules in sequence
  • To generate and issue CPD certificates
  • To process payments and issue invoices/receipts
  • To respond to support queries
  • To send programme updates (with your consent)
  • To maintain certificate verification records at learn.legacybe.ie/verify

5. Data Sharing

LBE does not sell personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase (database and authentication infrastructure, EU-West hosted)
  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Netlify (hosting infrastructure)
  • Irish Revenue Commissioners (VAT records, where legally required)
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies, where required by law

6. Data Retention

  • Account and progress data: retained for the duration of your access plus 3 years (to support CPD verification queries)
  • Certificate records: retained indefinitely (certificates are permanent and verifiable)
  • Payment records: retained for 7 years (Revenue/VAT compliance)
  • Marketing consent records: retained until consent is withdrawn

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data LBE holds about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — for any processing based on consent (e.g., marketing emails)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@legacybe.ie. LBE will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission (DPC) at dataprotection.ie.

8. Cookies

See our Cookie Policy for full details. The DC-LEARN platform uses strictly necessary cookies (session authentication) and no third-party advertising cookies.

9. Changes to this Policy

LBE may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be notified by email with 14 days’ notice. The current version is always available at legacybe.ie/privacy/.
