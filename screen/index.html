What a Screening Report contains

Ten working days · 2–3 pages · CEng-signed

01

Facility snapshot

IT load, PUE, grid connection, cooling topology, redundancy, reference year. Stated in the plain engineering terms a fund committee can read.

02

Compliance gap map

Where the facility sits against CRU/2025236 renewable obligation, EU Taxonomy PUE threshold (≤1.3), EED Article 12 reporting, and F-Gas exposure. Each gap named, sized, and tiered.

03

CRREM misalignment position

Indicative Misalignment Year against an LBE-derived DC pathway, disclosed as LBE-derived (CRREM Foundation does not publish a DC-specific pathway). Aligned to 1.5°C and 2°C scenarios.

04

Retrofit CAPEX and payback envelope

Screening-level CAPEX range (€/kW IT), indicative annual saving against canonical grid and carbon tax trajectories, simple payback, and a ten-year hold NPV at 8% discount.

05

Decision gate

A single clear recommendation: proceed to Desktop Assessment, proceed to disposal analysis, or continue compliance monitoring. No ambiguity. Signed off by a Chartered Engineer.

How we work

LBE identifies and quantifies. We do not design, specify, install, or commission the retrofit. When a facility needs engineering delivery, we refer to independent partner firms, we are never on both sides of the engagement.

All outputs are screening-level and indicative. Figures are tiered T1–T4 with sources named. Pricing is transparent and list-based: every client pays the same list price. No discounts are offered beyond time-limited Founding credits against list, disclosed on the invoice.
