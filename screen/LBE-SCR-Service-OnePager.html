DC Screening Report

A CEng-signed assessment of where your Irish data centre facility stands against EU Taxonomy, CRREM, CRU, and F-Gas obligations — with retrofit economics. Delivered in 10 working days from a desk.

A fund manager holding an Irish data centre asset faces six converging regulations — EU Taxonomy PUE thresholds, CRREM carbon pathways, CRU renewable obligations, F-Gas phase-down, EED reporting, and carbon tax escalation. Each one affects valuation, refinancing, and hold thesis. Getting a full engineering study takes 6 weeks and €30–50K. This report answers the same questions in 10 working days for €3,500, using client-supplied facility data and LBE canonical methodology. The output is a signed, sourceable document an investment committee can act on. LBE identifies and quantifies. Design and delivery remain with the appointed MEP firm.

What You Provide

  • Facility name and location
  • Year built
  • Total IT load (MW)
  • Rack count and density (kW/rack)
  • Current PUE (12-month actual)
  • Cooling type
  • Grid connection voltage and MIC
  • Renewable PPA share (%)
  • Generator fuel and annual hours

What We Assess

  • EU Taxonomy PUE alignment (≤ 1.3)
  • CRREM Misalignment Year (LBE-derived)
  • CRU 80% renewable obligation gap
  • Carbon intensity (location and market-based)
  • Carbon tax exposure now and 2030
  • F-Gas phase-down risk
  • EED Article 12 and 26 obligations
  • Retrofit economics (CAPEX, payback, NPV)

What You Receive

  • 2–3 page CEng-signed report
  • Carbon intensity — current and target
  • Misalignment Year (indicative)
  • Regulatory exposure summary (RAG)
  • Retrofit options with CAPEX ranges
  • 10-year hold model (base and stressed)
  • Decision gate — proceed or hold
  • Sourceable canonical data (T1–T4)

Carbon Position and CRREM Pathway

Current carbon intensity in kgCO₂/MWh_IT — location-based and market-based. Compared against LBE-derived CRREM data centre pathway bands. Misalignment Year calculated from current trajectory. Scope 1 (generators), Scope 2 (grid) stated explicitly with boundary disclosure.

Retrofit Economics — 10-Year Hold Model

CAPEX range for the primary retrofit path (cooling optimisation, PPA procurement, or HVO fuel switch). Annual energy saving, carbon saving, simple payback, and 10-year NPV at base and stressed discount rates. Presented as a hold thesis, not a design specification.
