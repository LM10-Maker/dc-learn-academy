WHAT CRREM ENABLES
Four ways CRREM drives action
From risk assessment to stakeholder dialogue, CRREM provides the data infrastructure the real estate sector needs.
01
Assess risks
Identify which buildings face transition risk and when, using science-based carbon pathways benchmarked to global climate targets.
02
Set targets
Anchor decarbonization targets to climate science — not arbitrary baselines — with pathways that reflect real building performance data.
03
Define roadmaps
Sequence retrofits, model costs, and plan capital expenditure against a clear, science-based trajectory for each asset and portfolio.
04
Facilitate dialogue
Give investors, managers, tenants, and regulators a shared language for discussing climate performance and transition planning.
