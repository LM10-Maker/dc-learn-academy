Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Plain English Summary

We use essential cookies to make the site work and anonymous analytics to understand how the programme is used. We don't use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or retargeting. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand usage patterns.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These are required for the programme to function. They cannot be disabled without breaking the experience.

  • Session state — remembers your progress within a module during a session
  • Authentication — maintains your login state for paid content (when Supabase auth is active)

Analytics Cookies

We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand which modules are most used, where learners spend time, and where they disengage. This data is anonymous and aggregated — we cannot identify individual users from it.

  • No personally identifiable information is collected
  • No cross-site tracking
  • No data shared with advertising networks

Cookies We Do Not Use

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Third-party behavioural analytics (e.g. Facebook Pixel, Google Ads)

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of the programme from functioning correctly. For instructions on managing cookies in your browser, visit aboutcookies.org.

4. Changes

If we introduce new categories of cookies, we will update this page and, where required, request your consent.
