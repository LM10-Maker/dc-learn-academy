A 15-chapter business novel set at fictional Clonshaugh DC, Dublin. Five professionals — an operator, a fund manager, an engineer, an ESG analyst, and a cost consultant — discover that eight regulatory clocks are already ticking on their building. Each chapter maps directly to a DC-LEARN module.

Written in the tradition of Goldratt’s The Goal — engineering reality through accessible narrative.

Who built this
Built by an engineer who does this for a living

DC-LEARN was built by Les Murphy CEng, MBA, MSc — a Chartered Engineer and MEP consultant with 30 years’ experience designing the power, cooling, and critical infrastructure systems that data centres are built from. Every module is grounded in the regulations, the engineering, and the commercial realities of the Irish DC market.

This is not a repackaged international course. Every standard cited, every calculation methodology, and every cost benchmark is Irish-specific — CRU, SEAI, ESB Networks, EPA, and the EU Taxonomy as applied in Ireland.
